  • For approach (2 x 106 cells/ 2ml): 554µl (+1446 µl DMEM)
 immediately afterwards

Cell culture

  • Container: [Cultivation container] 12 well plate prepared with [Buffer] HEPES I (isotonic) (or all other PBS)

 12:30

centrifugate for 5 min at 1,200 rpm using [Device] Eppendorf Centrifuge  12:31
suction fluid 12:36

incubate 30min in [Device] Eppendorf Thermomixer C (incubation shaker) at 37°C with 300rpm 12:40

prepare [Device] IonOptix C-Pace EM and [Device] LSM780:

  • switch on LSM
  • set hardware (objectives: water objective) & software (Ca-imaging "reuse")
  • switch on Argon-laser: 488nm
  • attach 12-well plate to the microscope set up
  • prepare [Device] IonOptix 12 well plate chamber (CD12-BDF / 03)
 immediately afterwards
centrifugate for 5 min at 1,200 rpm using [Device] Eppendorf Centrifuge  13:20
suction fluid
 13:25
add 250µl [Buffer] HEPES I (isotonic) (stained cell solution)
  
prepare [Cultivation container] 12 well plate (no LOT) with 1.5ml [Washing solution] PBS without Ca/Mg in all wells immediately afterwards
replace PBS from C2 well (position in chamber) and fill with 750µl [Buffer] HEPES I (isotonic) and 250µl of stained cell solution immediately afterwards
place [Device] IonOptix 12 well plate chamber electrodes on plate immediately afterwards
incubate for 10min without stimulation in LSM hood:

during incubation analyse with [Device] LSM780 and [Software] ZEN 2011 (black edition):

  • Stimulus: 488nm
  • Gain (master): 632
  • time series:
    • 150cycles (~ 5min, 1cycle = 2sec.)
