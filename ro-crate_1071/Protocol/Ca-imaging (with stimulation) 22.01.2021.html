add 1.5ml [Enzyme] Trypsin/EDTA  immediately afterwards
incubate 5min in [Device] SANYO CO2 Incubator at 37°C 8:35
stop the reaction with 3.5ml of culture medium and serum: 89% [Culture Medium] DMEM  + 10% [Serum] FCS + 1% [Antibiotic] Gentamicin  8:40
resuspend well to separate cells immediately afterwards
transfer the suspended cells into a [Cultivation container] Tube: 10ml (LOT E1701345) immediately afterwards
fill cup of [Device] CASY cell counter with 10ml [Buffer] CASYton: Schärfe System  immediately afterwards
take out 100µl of cell suspension into cup of [Device] CASY cell counter with buffer immediately afterwards

count cells with [Device] CASY cell counter and calculate 1,000,000 cells per ml for approach:

  • Cell count result: 2,044 x 105 cells/ml (96.47%) represents 3.679 x 106 cells/ml
  • For approach (2 x 106 cells/ 2ml): 544µl (+1456 µl DMEM)
 immediately afterwards 
centrifugate the suspended cells in [Cultivation container] Tube: 10ml  for 5 min at 1,200 rpm using [Device] Eppendorf Centrifuge  8:50
suction fluid 8:55
add 1ml of culture medium and serum: 89% [Culture Medium] DMEM  + 10% [Serum] FCS + 1% [Antibiotic] Gentamicin immediately afterwards
take out calculated volume for 2,000,000 cells in new [Cultivation container] Tube: 10ml (LOT E1701345) immediately afterwards
fill up to 2ml with culture medium and serum: 89% [Culture Medium] DMEM  + 10% [Serum] FCS + 1% [Antibiotic] Gentamicin (i.e. 526µl cell suspension and 1,474µl media) immediately afterwards
store in [Device] Eppendorf Thermomixer C (incubation shaker) at 37°C with 300rpm 9:00

Approach 1: Stimulation with 5V, 7.9Hz, 10ms

Cell culture

  • Container: [Cultivation container] 12 well plate prepared with [Buffer] HEPES I (isotonic) (or all other PBS)
  • Chamber: [Device] IonOptix 12 well plate chamber
  • Number of cells: 250,000 cells (chamber position: C2)

Note that chamber position and well plate position are different when attached!

Step Starting time
take out 250µl from cell suspension in new [Cultivation container] Tube: 10ml 
 9:00
wash cells with 1ml [Washing solution] PBS without Ca/Mg (#D8537-500ml, LOT RNBH6741): add PBS, swing container immediately afterwards
centrifugate for 5 min at 1,200 rpm using [Device] Eppendorf Centrifuge  9:01
suction fluid 9:06
add 1ml: 50% [Buffer] HEPES I (isotonic) + 50% [Buffer] HEPES II (hypotonic)  immediately afterwards
add 5µl [Indicator] Fluo-3/AM (make sure it is dark, no ambient light) immediately afterwards
incubate 30min in [Device] Eppendorf Thermomixer C (incubation shaker) at 37°C with 300rpm 9:10

prepare [Device] IonOptix C-Pace EM and [Device] LSM780:

  • switch on LSM
  • set hardware (objectives: water objective) & software (Ca-imaging "reuse")
  • switch on Argon-laser: 488nm
  • attach 12-well plate to the microscope set up
  • prepare [Device] IonOptix 12 well plate chamber (CD12-BDF / 03)
 immediately afterwards
centrifugate for 5 min at 1,200 rpm using [Device] Eppendorf Centrifuge  9:40
suction fluid
 9:45
add 250µl [Buffer] HEPES I (isotonic) (stained cell solution)
  
prepare [Cultivation container] 12 well plate (no LOT) with 1.5ml [Washing solution] PBS without Ca/Mg in all wells immediately afterwards
replace PBS from C2 well (position in chamber) and fill with 750µl [Buffer] HEPES I (isotonic) and 250µl of stained cell solution immediately afterwards
place [Device] IonOptix 12 well plate chamber electrodes on plate immediately afterwards
incubate for 10min with stimulation in LSM hood:
  • stimulation slot: 1
  • stimulation voltage: 5V
  • stimulation frequency: 7.9Hz
  • stimulation duration: 10ms

during incubation analyse with [Device] LSM780 and [Software] ZEN 2011 (black edition):

  • Stimulus: 488nm
  • Gain (master): 632
  • time series:
    • 150cycles (~ 5min, 1cycle = 2sec.)

Files: Calcium_Electro_22.01.2021:

  • 001_Zeitserie Stimulation_5V_7.9Hz.czi
 9:50
remove [Device] IonOptix 12 well plate chamber electrodes and place plate in LSM 10:00

analyse with [Device] LSM780 and [Software] ZEN 2011 (black edition):

  • Stimulus: 488nm
  • Gain (master): 632
  • time series:
    • 240cycles (~ 8min, 1cycle = 2sec.)
    • at 90th cycle added [Nucleotide] ATP (10µl, 100mM)

Files: Calcium_Electro_22.01.2021:

  • 001_Zeitserie nach Stimulation_5V_7.9Hz.czi
  • 001_Bild nach Stimulation_5V_7.9Hz.czi

 

  • Durch ATP-Zugabe hat sich der Bildausschnitt leicht verändert
  • Daten nur geteilt auswertbar (Bereich vor ATP-Zugabe <89. Zyklus; nach >90. Zyklus)
 immediately afterwards
